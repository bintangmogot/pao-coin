How to become Frenchie

1

Create a wallet

Download the MetaMask or Trust Wallet extension from the official website and add some ETH as fund.

2

Connect to DEX

Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet".

3

Swap ETH to FRENCH

To swap ETH for FRENCH, select ETH in the "from" field and choose FRENCH in the "to" field. Enter the amount and confirm.

4

Verify & Manage token

Verify your transaction status on Etherscan and manage token in your wallet extension.
